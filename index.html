Core Concepts of Accounting

? What is accountancy?

Accountancy is the systematic practice of recording, measuring, classifying, summarising, and communicating financial information about a business or organisation. It is both a profession and a discipline that underpins every financial decision made by businesses, governments, and individuals.

? What is the definition of accounting?

Accounting is the process of identifying, recording, and communicating the economic events of an organisation to interested users of the information.

Understanding ACCA

? What is ACCA?

ACCA stands for the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants. It awards the Chartered Certified Accountant designation globally.

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

? What is CA?

CA stands for Chartered Accountant. It is a professional designation awarded by country-specific accounting institutes.

ACCA vs CA Comparison Matrix

? Is ACCA better than CA?

Whether ACCA is "better" depends on your intended scope. ACCA offers immediate compliance access globally in 180+ countries, while CA functions heavily inside local jurisdictions.
